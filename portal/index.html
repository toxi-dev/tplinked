About

tplinked turns old OpenWrt routers into small offline network hubs.

Connecting

Join the router Wi-Fi, then open the router address in your browser.

Use cases

Events, classrooms, workshops, emergency notes, and local communication.

Hardware

Designed to stay lightweight for small routers like the TP-Link TL-WR740N.

File Notes

Save filenames, links, or notes without filling the router flash with uploads.
